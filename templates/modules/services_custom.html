App Features

Customer Reservation
Your customer can log in to the system with valid credentials and search for the required services from the list provided in the app. This feature enables your customer to view the list of services based on the categories like flights, cars, buses, and hotel etc, registered in the application. This feature lets them select the service and view the respective fare charts. Your customer can view and reserve/book the services based on the categories by making payment. Once the services are booked/reserved, they receive booking/reservation confirmation message by SMS / Email.
Online Ticket Reservation
Our travel software solutions allow your customer to view and book the tickets online from any location if registered in the app. This feature allows them to reserve the ticket online, where your customer can receive the e-tickets instantly by printing the ticket and downloading it. They will receive a booking confirmation message through SMS / Email.
Ticketing System
Our travel software development enables your customers to keep a track record of tickets confirmed/canceled and refunded. This feature helps the app owners in managing allocations which becomes hassle free and it also helps end users to reserve/book tickets and also by preventing inconvenience after booking until the trip gets executed.
Tours & Travel
This feature helps your customer to search for nearby tourist spots and travel agency or they can even search for a particular tour and travel packages based on city and location. Using this feature your customer can view the different list of tours and travel packages based on the search location and pricing as well. Moreover, they can view the details of each travel agent and the packages given by them, your customer can select and book the best tour and travel agents/packages and can make payments. Once the booking is confirmed your customer will receive details of the travel and tour agents, where they can contact them offline through contact number.
Routes
This feature gives a different list of tour and travel packages along with the display of routes. This feature allows your customer to enter source and destination where they will be able to view the maps and the route information of the entered location. Using this feature the app displays the maps with the help of the Google maps which will be integrated into the application. Using Google maps the system will display via route if the bus/train travels to the same location with a different route. This feature makes easy for your customer to understand the routes by Live tracking their Journey.
Hotel Booking Feature
This feature entails your customer to search and view the list of hotels based on the location and select the hotel from the list. Your customer can also search the hotel based on pricing. This feature helps them to view the list of amenities and helps in choosing the hotel as per the location with the details like hotel info, hotel overviews, Photo gallery, room type and amenities details, season/category wise room rates, cancellation policies, Offers discounts and promotions. Using this feature they can book the Hotel Room by adding the number of visitors the checking /checkout date, time and other basic details. Once the booking is done, your customer will receive a booking confirmation message by SMS / Email.
Tour Management
This feature enables your customer to search facilities based on various factors such as travel agent and packages, Transportation, Booking hotel and tour. They can view and select the services based on location, packages, pricing etc. Based on the selection, your customer can book the tour and avail the facilities by making payment and receive booking confirmation through SMS / Email. Using this feature the app owner can manage the details and track the information of the online customer, travel agent, packages, transport, hotel books, and tours.
Report Generation
Our travel booking software entails generation of reports of customer, travel agent, packages, transport, hotel books, and trips. Your customer can generate the reports on booking train, Bus Flight, Hotels etc. This feature helps them to search and filter reports on bookings. Using this feature your customer can easily download the PDF for booking train, Bus Flight, Hotels etc Application also provides excel export for booking services. They can view the reports in graphical representation based on the services.
